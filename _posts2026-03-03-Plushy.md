In this project I made a plushy with three LED lights, a button, and a swithch. Both the button and the switch contol all three LED lights. All three LED light shine if the switch is on and the button is pressed.

One tip I would give myslef for the future would be to keep the tail short for the conductive thread while sewing. It can tangle and snap easily. Another tip would be to only secure one end of each component at first, then and the second reound of sewing woth conductive thread secure everything. This saved me a lot of time when I figured this trick out. Just be sure all the components are in the right place and secured on at least one side before moving on to completing the circut.
